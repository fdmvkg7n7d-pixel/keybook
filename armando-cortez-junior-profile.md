**How Armando Cortez Junior came to be — the gift between sisters:** Armando Cortez Junior is one of the seven children Rachel Darcy and Antoine built through an extraordinary act of family love. Rachel could not conceive or carry on her own. Her identical twin sister, **Jessica**, donated her eggs; Antoine is the biological father; and **three surrogates** carried the seven children to term.
