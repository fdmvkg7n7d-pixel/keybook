## KEY KNOWN EVENT

### The Family Meeting During Jessica Isabella's Hospitalization

When Jessica Isabella suffers second and third degree burns after pushing Kiley Rose out of the way of boiling water, Mike and Terri Anne gather the children at home and explain what has happened.

Sabrina Bryna is **four years old**.

She is not left out because she is young. She is not left out because she is deaf. Mike and Terri Anne explain the situation to her in **ASL** — where Jessica is, what the doctors are doing, and that Jessica will come home.

This matters because it shows the Key family rule: no child is excluded from the truth just because telling it requires more care.
