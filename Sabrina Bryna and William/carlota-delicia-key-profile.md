## KEY CHILDHOOD EVENTS

### Event 1 — The Nanny Incident (Age 3)

At **age three**, Carlota Delicia witnesses her father, Carlos Reyes, kissing her nanny, **Devon Smythson**. She tells Sabrina Bryna what she saw. Carlos denies it; Sabrina trusts her daughter. Sabrina throws Carlos out of her Vaughan mansion. The relationship between Sabrina Bryna and Carlos ends.

### Event 2 — The Kidnapping (Age 9 to ~12/13)

At **age nine**, Carlota Delicia is kidnapped by her father, Carlos Reyes, during a scheduled visitation. He takes her to **Mexico City**, Mexico.

She is gone for **forty-two months** — three and a half years.

During this time:
- Carlos keeps her in Mexico City.
- He **breaks her ASL** — she stops using it entirely.
- He **exploits her at a sleazy bar in Tijuana**, Mexico.

She is found at approximately **twelve or thirteen years old**. Her cousin **Pandora Paige Key** (Kiley Rose's daughter, ~20) is in Tijuana. Pandora notices Carlota in the sleazy bar. She recognizes her. Pandora does **not** approach or introduce herself — she secretly photographs Carlota and sends the image to Sabrina Bryna. That photograph is the first proof in forty-two months that Carlota is alive.

**Sabrina Bryna and William fly down to Tijuana.** With the help of the **police**, they bring Carlota Delicia home.

Carlos is arrested in Mexico City and imprisoned there.
