## BIRTH & ORIGINS

Born ~2061 (Rachel & Antoine ~36), alongside the other triplets. The youngest. One of triplets.

**Annabella Nicki is one of triplets**, with **Melissa Lizette** and **Marcos Eduarte**. [Individual personality and how the three differ — TBD.]

**How Annabella Nicki came to be — the gift between sisters:** Annabella Nicki is one of the seven children Rachel Darcy and Antoine built through an extraordinary act of family love. Rachel could not conceive or carry on her own. Her identical twin sister, **Jessica**, donated her eggs; Antoine is the biological father; and **three surrogates** carried the seven children to term. Biologically, Annabella Nicki is Jessica's and Antoine's — but **Rachel is Annabella Nicki's mother**, fully and without qualification. This also makes Annabella Nicki a **biological half-sibling** (and social cousin) to Jessica's own daughters, **Samira Chaka and Sahara Hannan**.
