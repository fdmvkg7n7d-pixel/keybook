## BIRTH & ORIGINS

Born ~2059 (Rachel & Antoine ~34), alongside her identical twin. One of identical twins.

**Armella Beatrice is one of identical twins**, with **Kella Nakeisha**. They share a face and, like the family's other identical twins (Samira & Sahara; and a generation up, Jessica & Rachel), a bond the saga treats as central. [Individual personality and how the two differ — TBD.]

**How Armella Beatrice came to be — the gift between sisters:** Armella Beatrice is one of the seven children Rachel Darcy and Antoine built through an extraordinary act of family love. Rachel could not conceive or carry on her own. Her identical twin sister, **Jessica**, donated her eggs; Antoine is the biological father; and **three surrogates** carried the seven children to term. Biologically, Armella Beatrice is Jessica's and Antoine's — but **Rachel is Armella Beatrice's mother**, fully and without qualification. This also makes Armella Beatrice a **biological half-sibling** (and social cousin) to Jessica's own daughters, **Samira Chaka and Sahara Hannan**.
