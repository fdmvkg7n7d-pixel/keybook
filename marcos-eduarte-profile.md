Born ~2061 (Rachel & Antoine ~36), alongside the other triplets. One of triplets.

**Marcos Eduarte is one of triplets**, with **Melissa Lizette** and **Annabella Nicki**. [Individual personality and how the three differ — TBD.]

**How Marcos Eduarte came to be — the gift between sisters:** Marcos Eduarte is one of the seven children Rachel Darcy and Antoine built through an extraordinary act of family love. Rachel could not conceive or carry on her own. Her identical twin sister, **Jessica**, donated her eggs; Antoine is the biological father; and **three surrogates** carried the seven children to term. Biologically, Marcos Eduarte is Jessica's and Antoine's — but **Rachel is Marcos Eduarte's mother**, fully and without qualification. This also makes Marcos Eduarte a **biological half-sibling** (and social cousin) to Jessica's own daughters, **Samira Chaka and Sahara Hannan**.
